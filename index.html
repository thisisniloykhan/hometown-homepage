Where the sea meets the hills and history blends seamlessly with vibrant modern life.

Top three activities to do at Chattogram

🌊 Visit Patenga Sea Beach

Enjoy the calming sea breeze and beautiful sunsets at Patenga. It's a perfect place for evening walks, street food, and relaxing by the Bay of Bengal.

🏞️ Explore Foy's Lake

Spend a fun day at this scenic lake surrounded by hills. You can enjoy boat rides, amusement park activities, or simply relax in nature.

⛰️ Hike to Batali Hill

Climb the highest hill in the city for a panoramic view of Chattogram. It's a great spot for photography and peaceful sunsets.
